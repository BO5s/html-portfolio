The Best Movies According to Ian

My Top 3 Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) Movies or TV Series

Avengers: Endgame

Culminating the Infinity saga, the battle scenes alone provide the epic reason for why this is my #1.

Loki

Brings to life the transformation of a fan-favorite in Loki from god of mischief to god of stories in a mind-bending adventure.

What If...?

The reimagining of characters' stories brings fresh emotions and perspectives to familiar fan favorites, even time-bending twists that affect the MCU.
